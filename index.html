About Darning Needle Knits

Darning Needle Knits is a personal project created by Julia Kent, an environmental scientist, software engineer, passionate fantasy reader, and dedicated knitter in the front range of Colorado. With a love for crafting and community, Julia launched this brand to foster connections and share the art of knitting with others through lessons and bringing nature-inspired handcrafted knitwear and decorative pieces to local craft fairs.
