About Darning Needle Knits

Darning Needle Knits is a personal project created by Julia Kent, an environmental scientist, software engineer, passionate fantasy reader, and dedicated knitter. With a love for crafting and community, Julia launched this market to bring beautifully handcrafted knitwear and decorative pieces to local craft fairs, fostering connections and sharing the art of knitting with others.
